ABOUT

XANADU HONG KONG LIMITED was established on May 19, 2011, and has an independent office in Tianjin Economic and Technological Development Zone, Binhai New Area, Tianjin. With strong domestic of China development momentum, our company has gradually developed into an international trading company with a complete import and export system and multiple resources.

ABOUT US

Our company has a board of directors, general manager’s office, marketing department, finance department, operations business department and security risk assessment department, and more than 30 employees with professional international trade knowledge and practical experience.

The mineral business is mainly engaged in chrome and manganese ore import business, our company deal with chrome ore resources from Albania，South Africa, Madagascar, Zimbabwe ,Turkey and other countries, and we has good upstream procurement channels in relevant countries. As for Manganese ore, we usually import the cargo from Brazil, South Africa, Australia, Senegal, Gabon and so on.

Other products such as Celestite ore, Gilsonite, Bitumen(70 #, 90 # etc.) of the import and export which involved Spain, Turkey, Albania, Indonesia, the Middle East, Russia , and other countries and regions. We can supply Celestite ore around 30000MT per month and Bitumen around 50000 MT per month, with stable upstream channels.

To ensure the quality of the cargoes, we cooperate with the world well-known inspection companies such as CCIC, ALS and so on. Since the establishment of our company, we adhere to the "integrity first, customer first" business philosophy, and maintain good relationship with customers. Also the spirit of "pragmatic, forge ahead" leads us to create the core competitiveness of our enterprise.
